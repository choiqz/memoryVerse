Privacy Policy

Effective June 11, 2026

memoryVerse is a Bible verse memorization app. It is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking SDKs.

Microphone & speech recognition

memoryVerse uses your device's microphone so you can recite verses aloud. Your speech is converted to text by your device's built-in speech recognition service (Apple Speech on iOS, Google speech services on Android) and scored on your device. memoryVerse itself never records, stores, or transmits your audio or transcripts anywhere. The device's speech service processes audio according to its platform's own privacy policy (Apple, Google). You can deny or revoke microphone permission at any time in your device settings; the app then falls back to typed recitation.

Your data stays on your device

Your verse library, review history, streaks, and settings are stored in a local database on your device only. Deleting the app permanently deletes all of this data. We have no servers and no way to access it.

Feedback

If you choose to use the "Send Feedback" option, it opens your email app with a pre-filled message that includes basic diagnostics (app version, platform, and the number of verses in your library). Nothing is sent unless you send the email yourself, and the information is used only to respond to your feedback.

Children's privacy

memoryVerse does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
